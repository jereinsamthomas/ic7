About The Clan

Where the clan
becomes family.

IC7 Iconic Sevens started in 2020 when a handful of eFootball players from different corners of Kerala found each other online and started grinding matches together. What began as pickup games between strangers turned into a clan with a name, a crest and a reputation.

Today we're a full-blown eFootball community — players from every district competing together, climbing coin tournaments for free, and repping IC7 in clan championships across the scene.

We play to win, but IC7 is really about family. Join the clan and you're backed by people across Kerala who've got your back — as long as you hold up your end: the criteria to join are strict, and once you're in, the rules are non-negotiable.
